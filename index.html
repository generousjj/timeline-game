❓ How to Play

×

1. Choose a subject from the dropdown menu and click "Load Game".

2. Cards with events will appear in the deck area.

3. Drag and drop the cards in the correct chronological order in the play area.

4. If you're unsure of an event's date, you can click "Skip" to move to the next card.

5. Use "Back" to restore a skipped card if needed.

6. When you have at least 2 cards placed, click "Check Results" to see your accuracy.
